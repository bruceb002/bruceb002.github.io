About Me

Hi, my name is Bruce Bai. I am a Junior at UC Santa Cruz majoring in Computer Science. I have experience interning at Best Egg, a financial company focused on providing flexible loan and credit card services to its customers, in the technical team. I also have experience with a hackathon, in which I worked with my team to produce a rideshare website aimed for UC Santa Cruz students to use.

For my first internship at Best Egg, I worked in the financial loss team by migrating code from the SAS system to the SQL system. In addition to migrating the code, I ended up needing to do data checking and cleaning by adding my own code and implenting new logic. At the end, I was able to speed up the process by approximately 20 times.

For my second internship at Best Egg, I worked with the tech team by helping migrate between different chatbot services. I also helped with the migration of deployment services.

For the hackathon, my team used Google Maps API to gather trip data and allow the user to view a map of their trip route. My part in this project was to program the entirety of the main page - where users have the ability to view their trips. I needed to pull the trip information from the database we used and do some calculations as nessecary. It took us two days and one all nighter to finish the project. At the end, we won first place as our project appeared to have had the most potential for an official service, and adhered to the theme of the competition the best (help solve a issue either locally at UCSC or globally so that the sense of unity among a community may be improved).
